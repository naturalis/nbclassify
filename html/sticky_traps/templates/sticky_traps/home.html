OrchID

Welcome to OrchID, an orchid photo identifier. This web application allows you to identify a photo of an orchid up to the species level. Currently, only the slipper orchids (subfamily Cypripedioideae) can be recognized. Use the form below to upload your photo.

The photo needs to be reasonably standardized for the identification to work properly. Check that your photo:

  • has a frontal view of the flower;
  • shows at least one non-overlapping flower;
  • has a background with good contrast to the flower;
  • is not too small (at least 400 pixel width and height).
